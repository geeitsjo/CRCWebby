Who We Are

Wellington CRC, formerly the Christian Revival Centre, has been in Wellington for over sixty years. It has a stable history going right back to the roots of the Pentecostal movement, even as far back as 1926 when Smith Wigglesworth first came to New Zealand and the Pentecostal full gospel message was preached. Our church consists of a broad range of cultures and age groups and reflects the changing nature of New Zealand culture and community. We enjoy a healthy growing church made up of passionate and committed people making up this vibrant community.

We are a Pentecostal church in the heart of Wellington New Zealand. We celebrate Communion weekly and encourage people to not only participate in church services but also to connect with people and build friendship and relationship on an ongoing and long term basis.

Our focus is to become by grace what Christ is by nature. This means we are always encouraging people to grow and become a mature disciple of Jesus Christ. A lifelong journey for everyone.

Mission

Our mission is to be the doorway to God's healing, restoration and transformation secured through Christ's death, burial, and resurrection, for ALL people. (1 Timothy 4:10) To see people become Christ-like through God's grace and transforming Holy Spirit.

In doing this we fulfill the great commission to make disciples and the great commandment to love God and love people.

  • We are a worshipping Church centred on God and His purposes.
  • We are a family , called to love people.
  • We are a hospital , called to bring healing through the love of Christ.
  • We are a school , called to train and equip God's people for service.
  • We are an army , called to release God's people into service as He directs us.
  • We are disciples helping others to become disciples.

Wellington CRC is a member church of the Christian Revival Churches New Zealand and a registered charitable institution in New Zealand.


Vision

Our vision is to be a healthy Christian Church Reflecting and outworking God's mission of love to all people. To become by grace what Christ is by nature.
